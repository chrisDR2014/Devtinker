    Who Am I?

    My name is Christoffer Carlsson. I'm originally from Sweden but I have been living in the Dominican Republic for the past 12 years. I first visited this beautiful country in February 2011 for vacation, and then one year later I moved here after I meet my wife. I have been living here since then and I am very happy with my new home. In 2019 I aquired Dominican Citizenship so now I'm both Swedish and Dominican.

    For the past 12 years, life in the Dominican Republic has been a vibrant blend of culture and warmth. Mornings filled with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, afternoons spent on pristine beaches, and evenings alive with merengue rhythms. From exploring colonial Santo Domingo to savoring traditional mangu, every moment is a celebration of this beautiful island life.
